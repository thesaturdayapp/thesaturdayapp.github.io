0.3.0

January 7, 2019

  • New Features
  • Bug Fixes
  • Improvements

Saturday v0.3.0 is the greatest app we have ever made.

Improvements in Groups

In addition to private groups, you can now start public groups. Anybody can join public groups and contribute videos. Public groups cannot be made private once created. Finally, the Home screen was completely redesigned to organize groups and improve the efficiency of video uploads.

Changes to Profile

The user profile is now private by default; other users will not be able to see your liked videos or the number of followers you have.

Saved Videos

There is now an option to save videos locally. These videos are not uploaded, and instead will appear only on your Profile page on your device. If you log out or delete the app, you will lose these saved videos.

New Filters

This version includes three new filters, namely Dim, C7T, and Glitch. (Note: filters are subject to frequent change.)

Bug Fixes

We fixed a ton of bugs throughout the app and achieved an experience that's as smooth as butter.
